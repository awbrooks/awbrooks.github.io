Projects

- Vanderbilt Microbiome Initiative - Co-Investigator -

Vanderbilt's first microbiome clinical trial examining the effects of a western to vegetarian dietary shift on oral and gut microbial communities.

- The Microbiome as a Mediator of Health Disparities - Thesis Project -

We are investigating whether ethnic microbiome variation may be an underlying factor influencing health disparity outcomes.

- Phylosymbiosis: Eco-Evolutionary Microbiome Assembly -

We demonstrated across 31 animal species that the evolutionary relationships of hosts are concordant with the ecological relatedness of their microbiomes.
